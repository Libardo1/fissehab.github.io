Hi, I am Fisseha. I love developing data products and solving challenging real world problems using data.

I am a data scientist at Aurotech and PhD candidate at Johns Hopkins University. My PhD study is in atmospheric physics. For the last six years, I have been analyzing large volume climate and hydrology data, using various statistical data analysis techniques, to understand physical processes in the atmosphere and to evaluate how climate change may impact the Nile River flow, which is the longest river in the world.
